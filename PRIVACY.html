Privacy Policy for VigilantEar 👂🛰️

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

Introduction

VigilantEar ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

VigilantEar processes data entirely on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal data to our servers.

The app may access the following information locally:

  • Microphone audio – Used in real-time to detect sounds (sirens, vehicles, etc.).
  • Location data – Used to display the direction and estimated distance of detected sounds on the map.
  • Device orientation and motion – Used to improve directional accuracy.

How We Use Your Information

All processing happens on-device. We use this information solely to provide real-time acoustic awareness and directional guidance.

We do not:

  • Store audio sound streams or recordings
  • Send any PII data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Share your information with third parties

Data Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect or transmit any data.

On-Device Processing

VigilantEar is designed with privacy in mind. All sound detection, location processing, and map rendering happens locally on your iPhone. No personally identifiable information (PII) leaves your device.

We may log application exceptions to the cloud without any PII data or sound streams (such as when a new sound type is detected that the ML chip added but isn't optimized within our application yet).

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal data, there is no need to request deletion or access. You remain in full control of your data at all times.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
