Privacy Policy for VigilantEar

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

Introduction

VigilantEar ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

VigilantEar processes data entirely on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal data to our servers.

The app may access the following information locally:

  • Microphone audio – Used in real-time to detect sounds (sirens, vehicles, etc.).
  • Location data – Used to display the direction and estimated distance of detected sounds on the map.

How We Use Your Information

All processing happens on-device. We use this information solely to provide real-time acoustic awareness and directional guidance.

Data Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect or transmit any data.
